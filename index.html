Who I Am

Bridging Data, Policy & Real-World Impact

I'm Obed Nyangena — an economist and policy consultant with over 12 years of experience designing data-driven solutions across climate adaptation, food security, health, sustainable development, digitalization, and national security.

I currently lead Research, Strategy and Planning at the National Mining Corporation, where I spearheaded Kenya's first green minerals strategic plan — positioning the country as a critical minerals hub for the global energy transition, aligned with the Paris Agreement and UN SDGs.

My track record spans international policy advisory with the World Bank, USAID, IOM, and the EU — and I bring rigorous econometric methods together with sharp policy instincts to every engagement. When I'm not in the data, I'm writing op-eds and contributing to national policy conversations.
